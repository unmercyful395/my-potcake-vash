Potcake Trivia

Where did Potcakes get their name?

Potcake is the name given to the dogs of the Bahamas and the Turks & Caicos Islands. It came about because the locals fed the caked remains of the cooking pot to the dogs. The Bahama's Kennel Club recognizes the Potcake as a breed. (Source: Potcake Place)
